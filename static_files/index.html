Hello to everyone! This website is about ISAAC ASIMOV and his works.

Isaac Asimov is my favorite author. In my opinion, he is one of the best science-fiction authors of all time. As mentioned in the article "Isaac Asimov's Biography: The Pebble in the Sky", his books really inspired me to take up dare to dream of the future of mankind and helped me to understand the Universe, Galaxies, Stars, etc. I can even say that his books changed my perspective on life and they helped me to look wider and wisely to the life.

He creates a universe in his books, and most of his books (actually all his books which I have read) helps to form a part of this universe. You can find yourself in a great adventure.

Due to all of these reasons, I really admire him and his works. Therefore, I wanted to introduce him to you and I would highly recommend everyone to read his books.
